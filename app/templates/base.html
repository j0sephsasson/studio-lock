Frequently Asked Questions

To book a studio session, simply navigate to the booking page on our website and select the date and time that you would like to reserve. Follow the prompts to complete the booking process and pay for your session.
Payment for studio sessions are handled securely via Stripe.
If you need to cancel or reschedule your booking, you can do so by contacting the studio directly or by reaching out to our customer support team. Please note that cancellation policies may vary by studio, so be sure to read the terms and conditions carefully before booking.
Each studio's equipment list is displayed on the studio's listing page on our platform. If you have specific equipment requirements, we recommend reaching out to the studio directly to confirm that they have what you need.
The availability of each studio is stored in our database. Simply select the date and time that you are interested in to see if the studio is available.
